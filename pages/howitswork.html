Automatic watering

Self-watering

Yes, it's self-watering. When it comes to watering your plant, feel free to forget for a week or even a month! Plant Hero does the job for you. How? Water is held in the tank by atmospheric pressure. It will gradually seep out onto the receded tray, and then reabsorbed by your plant through a special ceramic medium. Your one job is to refill the water tank whenever it gets low.
